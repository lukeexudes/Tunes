About

Tunes is a free Discord music bot that delivers high-quality music to your Discord server. You can easily add the music bot to your Discord server with zero configuration.

YouTube Functions

Search

Instead of linking directly to a track, you can also choose to simply just type in the name of your song and let Tunes find it for you.

Shuffle and Repeat

Tunes has full support for shuffling your playlist. You can even choose to put just one or the entire queue on repeat.
